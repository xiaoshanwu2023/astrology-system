The month opens quietly, with the same kind of silence as you might experience when snow is falling—a sense of lovely peacefulness. You will want privacy and rest as the month begins. You’ve been handling more responsibilities now that Saturn is firmly settled in Aries (as of February 13) to stay a little over two years, until April 12, 2028.

You might imagine being in a luxury hotel suite with your child or children, relaxed, happy, having help from your mother or a nanny, and ordering room service while you hold the remote to the TV. The idea of having help and not needing to lift a finger to do anything sounds so good but impossibly out of reach for the moments. I completely understand.

If you have children, ask a friend or relative to watch and play with your children over the weekend for a few hours so you can catch up on sleep. If you have too much to do at work, ask if you can hire an extra person. Even if you are told that person would have to be a temporary worker because the budget won’t allow for adding a permanent staff member, having her or him in your group could help to move your current project forward quicker and relieve some of the pressure on you. Although you could say that training the person so they could be of real help sounds exhausting, it could be worth it. Besides, that person might be a quick learner and surprise you with how they can help you.

The full moon of March 3 is a lunar (full moon) eclipse in Virgo, 13 degrees. I wrote about it in depth last month, but I will reiterate what this eclipse is likely to bring. This eclipse will draw your attention to your health, both in terms of your mental and physical well-being. This eclipse will also focus you on your work-a-day environment and the projects on your desk.
